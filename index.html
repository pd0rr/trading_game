Welcome to Price Action! A game about trading the financial markets.

Press Buy to purchase shares and Sell to sell them. You can buy on leverage or take a short position without any limitations.

Press Update to advance to the next time step. Press Play to advance time automatically. Press Pause to pause the game. You can buy and sell shares even when the game is paused.

The chart at the top represents the stock price as a function of time. Try to predict the stock's price and make money by trading! The chart on the bottom displays your balance as a yellow line.

A computer opponent is trading on the same market; its balanced is displayed as a purple line.

The goal of the game is to make more money than the computer within the next 500 time steps. Good luck!
