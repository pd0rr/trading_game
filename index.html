Welcome to Price Action! Press 'Buy' to purchase shares and 'Sell' to sell them. In this level, you can buy on leverage or take a short position without any limitations.

Press 'Update' to advance to the next time step. Press 'Play' to advance time automatically. Press 'Pause' to pause the game. You can buy and sell shares even when the game is paused.

The chart on the top represents the stock price as a function of time. Try to predict the stock's price and make money by trading! The chart on the bottom displays your balance as a yellow line. A computer opponent is also trying to make money by trading; its balanced is displayed as a purple line.

The goal of the game is to acquire a higher balance than the computer player within the next 500 time steps. Good luck!
